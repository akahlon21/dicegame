Dice Game

Rules:

  • Roll a pair of dice for the player and another pair for the computer.
  • Each player rolls three times.
  • If any of the dice comes up as a 1, the score for that round is 0.
  • If a player rolls a pair of the same numbers, their score is the total of the two dice times 2.
  • If a player rolls any other combination of dice, their score is the total value of the two dice.
  • After three rolls, the total scores are compared, and the player with the highest score wins.
  • Click "Roll Dice" to play, and "Reset Game" to start over.
